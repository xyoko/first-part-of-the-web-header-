Blueberry Pancakes Breakfast

Submitted by chef_master • Nov 22, 2025

Fluffy pancakes loaded with fresh blueberries and maple syrup. Perfect for a weekend breakfast!

20 min 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions:

1. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl. 2. Whisk together eggs, milk, and melted butter. 3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed. 4. Fold in blueberries gently. 5. Cook on a hot griddle until bubbles form. 6. Flip and cook until golden brown.

Thai Green Curry Dinner

Submitted by cooking_enthusiast • Nov 21, 2025

Aromatic Thai curry with chicken, vegetables, and coconut milk. Rich and flavorful!

35 min 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons green curry paste
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • Thai basil leaves
  • Fish sauce to taste

Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a wok over medium-high heat. 2. Add curry paste and fry until fragrant. 3. Add chicken and cook until sealed. 4. Pour in coconut milk and bring to a simmer. 5. Add vegetables and cook until tender. 6. Season with fish sauce and garnish with basil.
